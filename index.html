About

Hi! I'm a researcher interested in multimodality, world models and physical AI. I am currently a student researcher at NVIDIA's GEAR Lab as the only undergraduate, working on SOTA robotics models, interactive world models, and everything in-between. I owe most of my success for people who have believed in me and taken time out to mentor me, particularly, Kezhen Chen, Joel Jang, & Jim Fan.

Previously...

I joined Together AI during Series A, researching on compute-efficient architecutres for post-training LLM's to become natively multimodal (perception and generation), advised by Kezhen Chen.

At the Stanford AI Lab, I researched on lambda-calculus based proof system with a finite action space, making RL tractable and enabling agents to discover tactics and emergent curricula, advised by Gabriel Poesia and Noah Goodman

I spent a summer teaching LLM's how to conduct geometric reasoning and solve euclidia.xyz and advised by Henryk Michalewski and Mateusz Malinowski.

I fell in love with proof-writing and dedicated my high school life to it, competing in various international olympiads for Pakistan.
